SPARTANS is a non-profit community. We are trying to reduce the stress and anxiety among youth by bringing several multinational companies under a single community and focus to provide jobs to many people according to their qualities. we also introduce a new one-on-one chat room concept.According to recent researches, anxiety and depression are increasing among youth because of the lack of proper communication and guidelines. So as to improve the communication and to guide them on their problems without judging, revealing their identity, betraying their trust and respecting their privacy. They can find their own solutions from volunteers who are qualified to handle the situation. The volunteers will be youngsters so they can relate the issues. This is a one-to-one private chat room. We support them to speak up. This will help them open up with parents or trustworthy adults by understanding the intensity of their own issue. By this, we can provide emotional support to our future assets and we can be an asset to our people.

Inspired from the ‘Big brother big sister’ system of America, we can introduce ´let’s talk’ to our country to provide a positive adult role model and mentor to these children to develop a one-to-one relationship with a youth by offering consistency, encouragement, and role modelling The program will be in the form of a mobile application through which the volunteers, who join the program after different vetting processes, communicate with youth who sign-up into the system. They will have a consistent relation and provide a safe space for the youth to share their insecurities and seek guidance.
